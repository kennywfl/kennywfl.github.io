Basic Information

  • Total 8 waves.
  • Map is huge, got 24 catapult for travelling fast around the map.
  • Get chaotic very easily, so do not let your guard down.
  • Have the new weapon called A.I.S (Arks Interception Silhouette) at your disposal.
  • Have new darker enemy that will put infection socket.
  • Have Darker Walls that will block or isolate you from others.
  • Have Infected Socket Tower that will charge up laser and deal huge damage to the tower.
  • Have Infected Plane that will deal huge damage to players and also towers.
  • Unlike previous MBD1 or MBD2, every single bosses in MBD3 will have their own individual drops.

Tower infection

  • Picture above shows the infection time for each tower in each wave.
  • Unlike previous MBD, tower infection starts appear on first wave of MBD3.
  • Infection time will vary in about ~2 seconds
  • Time from infection started until the infection core came out will have about ~8 seconds time.

Crystals

  • Crystal is crucial in MBD3, as it’s the main thing to allow players to use A.I.S and various useful stuffs like barrier and heal.
  • Target for crystal should be at least 7000 or 10000 in order to utilize 2 or 3 A.I.S.
  • There are 3 different types of crystal in the field as the wave proceed, each which gives different points:
    Wave Crystal Points
    1 ~ 3 10 Points
    4 ~ 6 20 Points
    7 ~ 8 30 Points
  • Crystal are all around the map, but the four corners of the map will have a higher numbers of crystal, refer to the above map for a clearer picture.
  • However, in later waves, corners are often blocked by darker wall.
  • Recommended crystal to gather before wave end are as follow:
    Wave Target Point
    1 300~500
    2 1000
    3 2000
    4 4000
    5 5000
    6 7000
    7 8500~10000

A.I.S Manual and Trivial

  • A.I.S last for 2 minutes once summoned, or 120 sec to be exact, it will be colored based on your cloth’s color.
  • A.I.S is the main succeed point for MBD3, so it’s important to know and use it correctly.
  • The priority target of A.I.S should be roughly like this:
    • Infected plane > Infected socket tower > parasite > groups of enemies on tower > bosses > walls
  • Most, if not all A.I.S attacks can be Perfect Striked for extra damage.
  • Use photon cannon on a pack of mobs or highly dangerous boss like hunar.
  • Do not use photon cannon on single boss only, try to align to shot a group of enemies.
  • A.I.S can pick up crystal and items also, but will need some positioning.
  • Below is the weapon/action list and the pp cost of each action:
    Action PP cost Description
    Jump 0 pp Space for jump, hold to have a longer jump, can double jump also
    Dash 15 pp F for dash, tapping quickly can result in multi dash, very useful in moving a huge distance
    Left click 0 pp Rapid fire rifle attack, can be used to recover pp by shotting tower
    Right click 15 pp Photon Sword slashing, 3rd hit deal a huge damage compare to 1st and 2nd hit
    Weapon Action + Left Click 55 pp Fire 3 missiles to the targer, it will home if locked on anything
    Weapon Action + Right Click 70 pp Dashing Photon Sword slash, useful when targeting a groups of mobs
    Sub palette 0 0 pp Displaying the times remaining for A.I.S and also exit the A.I.S when pressed, exiting makes A.I.S disappear
    Sub palette 1 0 pp Photon Particle Cannon, can be only used once per A.I.S ride, deal huge damage to enemy, do switch to manual aim for maximum killing result
    Sub palette 2 0 pp Close range sphere like explosion that freeze enemies like tower barrier
